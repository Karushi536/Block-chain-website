How It Works

Understanding the Voting Process

The JOKA Voting App is designed to simplify the voting process, ensuring that your vote is secure and counted accurately. Follow the steps below to understand how to cast your vote using our app.

Step 1: Register

Register for an account using your personal details. Ensure that your information is accurate to avoid any issues during the voting process.

Step 2: Verify Your Identity

Verify your identity through the app's secure verification process. This ensures that each vote is legitimate and that the voting process is secure.

Step 3: Select the Position

Choose the position you want to vote for (e.g., President, Governor, Senate, MP, MCA). The app will guide you through the available positions one by one.

Step 4: Choose a Candidate

Browse through the list of candidates for the selected position. Click on a candidate's photo to select them, then confirm your vote by clicking the 'Vote' button.

Step 5: Review and Confirm

Review your selections to ensure they are correct. Once you are satisfied, submit your votes. You will receive a confirmation message once your votes have been successfully submitted.

Step 6: Confirmation

After voting, you will see a confirmation page with a 'Thumbs Up' image, thanking you for participating in the democratic process.
